Binary Operators

A Binary Operator is an operator that operates and manipulates them to return a result (output). Operators are represented by special characters or by keywords and provide an easy way to compare numerical valus or character strings. There are various binary operators such as "and", "or", "not", "xor".

The following are examples of operatoions
